Arc Max

Arc Max is Arc's first bundle of AI features. We prioritized building useful AI that made people's browsing subtly better without asking them to learn anything new.

I got to work with an extraordinary team of engineers — Rosey Eisenbrand and Nathan Tannar — and had the pleasure of working closely with Patrick Moberg to prototype so many more things than we eventually launched. Karla Cole came up with a fantastic brand, and designed the settings screen.

Some day I'll write more about it. For now, you can hear about it from The Verge or Techcrunch.
